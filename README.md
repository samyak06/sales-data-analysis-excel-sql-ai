# Business Performance Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes sales data using Excel, SQL, and AI to identify product performance, regional trends, and profitability.  
The goal is to move beyond basic reporting and support practical business decision-making.

## Tools Used
- Excel → Data cleaning and dashboard creation  
- SQL → Data analysis and querying  
- AI → Generating insights and decision support  

## Key Insights
- Laptop is the top-performing product in both revenue and profit  
- Mobile generates steady revenue but has lower profit efficiency  
- Shoes show strong profit margins despite lower sales volume  
- Watch contributes the least and lacks strong market presence  
- North and West regions perform consistently better than others  

## Business Decisions
- Continue focusing on Laptop as a stable revenue driver while managing inventory carefully  
- Improve Mobile profitability through pricing or cost optimization  
- Gradually scale Shoes due to strong margin potential  
- Limit investment in Watch until performance improves  
- Prioritize North and West regions while testing improvements in weaker regions  

## Role of AI in This Project
AI was used to interpret data and generate structured business insights.  
While traditional analysis focuses on numbers and queries, AI helps in:
- Converting raw results into clear insights  
- Identifying patterns quickly  
- Supporting decision-making with minimal manual effort  

This reflects a growing shift where analysts combine technical skills with AI to improve efficiency and clarity.

## Conclusion
This project demonstrates how combining Excel, SQL, and AI can lead to better understanding of business performance and more practical decision-making.
